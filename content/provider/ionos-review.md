# IONOS Review (2026)

IONOS is a European hosting company with budget VPS and business hosting solutions.  
It combines affordable base pricing with business-oriented offerings and service integrations suitable for small businesses.

## Market Position

IONOS sits between shared hosting and VPS/cloud:

- Strong business hosting product set  
- Affordable entrance pricing  
- Simple control panel

It’s often chosen by:

- Small business owners  
- WordPress beginners  
- Agencies that want simple hosting with support

- Low introductory rates (~$2–3/mo)

Typical VPS plans scale up moderately but remain cost-effective.

IONOS data centers focus on:
