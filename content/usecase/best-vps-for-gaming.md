## What is the best VPS for gaming?

The best VPS for gaming is not the cheapest — it is the one with:

- Lowest RTT to players
- Stable routing (low jitter)
- Minimal packet loss
- Consistent p95 latency

For Asia gaming traffic, **Tokyo and Singapore** are common stable choices.  
For North America, **Los Angeles** is often preferred.

## What matters more than location?

1. Routing stability  
2. Jitter (variance)  
3. Packet loss  
4. ISP-level congestion  
5. Peak-hour performance  

For multiplayer games, jitter often impacts experience more than average latency.

---

## Gaming server checklist

1. Test from real player ISPs using MTR / WinMTR
2. Measure p95 latency, not just average
3. Avoid oversold VPS plans
4. Choose high-frequency CPU plans if available
5. Monitor continuously during peak hours

---

## Should you use a CDN for gaming?

CDNs help static assets and downloads, but real-time gameplay depends on direct server latency.  
Choose region first, CDN second.

---

## Final recommendation

Start by selecting the region closest to the majority of players.  
Validate routing stability before scaling.

Low latency is achieved through testing — not assumptions.
