# OVHcloud Review (2026)

OVHcloud is one of Europe’s largest independent cloud and dedicated infrastructure providers.  
Unlike many competitors, OVHcloud owns and operates its own data centers and hardware infrastructure, giving it tighter cost control and vertical integration.

For European deployments, cost-focused SaaS, hosting resellers, and dedicated server workloads, OVHcloud is one of the strongest price-to-performance options in 2026.

## Market Position

OVHcloud is:

- One of the largest European cloud infrastructure providers  
- Strong in VPS and dedicated servers  
- Cost-competitive against AWS and DigitalOcean in EU regions  
- Popular among hosting resellers and SaaS startups  

Compared to hyperscale clouds:

- Smaller global footprint than AWS/GCP/Azure  
- Stronger cost efficiency in Europe  
- Fewer managed service layers  

OVHcloud positions itself as a **cost-effective alternative to hyperscalers**.

## Pricing Breakdown (Realistic View)

OVHcloud pricing is generally predictable and resource-dense.

### VPS Plans (Typical)

| Plan | CPU | RAM | Storage | Bandwidth | Price |
|------|------|------|----------|------------|--------|
| VPS Starter | 1 vCPU | 2 GB | 20 GB SSD | 100 Mbps | ~$5/mo |
| VPS Value | 2 vCPU | 4 GB | 40 GB SSD | 250 Mbps | ~$10/mo |
| VPS Essential | 4 vCPU | 8 GB | 80 GB SSD | 500 Mbps | ~$20/mo |

Bandwidth is usually included up to a cap, often generous relative to price.

### Dedicated Servers

OVHcloud is particularly strong in dedicated infrastructure:

- High-core-count servers  
- NVMe storage options  
- Competitive pricing compared to US-based providers  

Dedicated offerings often provide superior raw performance per dollar.

## Infrastructure & Data Centers

OVHcloud owns and operates multiple data centers, primarily in:

- France  
- Germany  
- UK  
- Poland  
- Canada  
- United States  

Ownership of physical infrastructure allows:

- Lower pricing volatility  
- Direct control over hardware lifecycle  
- Strong DDoS mitigation baked into the network  

For EU latency-sensitive applications, OVHcloud performs extremely well.

## FAQ

### Is OVHcloud cheaper than DigitalOcean?
In most comparable EU VPS tiers, yes — OVHcloud typically offers more RAM or bandwidth for similar pricing.

### Is OVHcloud good for SaaS?
Yes, particularly for EU-focused SaaS products that prioritize cost efficiency and performance.

### Does OVHcloud include DDoS protection?
Yes — anti-DDoS protection is included at the network level.

### Does OVHcloud offer managed Kubernetes?
Yes, but the ecosystem is smaller compared to hyperscale cloud providers.

### Is OVHcloud suitable for global deployments?
It can work for moderate global workloads, but hyperscale providers offer broader geographic redundancy.

### Is OVHcloud good for dedicated servers?
Yes — dedicated infrastructure is one of OVHcloud’s strongest segments.
