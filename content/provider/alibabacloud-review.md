## FAQ

### Is Alibaba Cloud good outside China?
Yes — Alibaba Cloud is particularly strong in **Asia-Pacific** (e.g., Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia). For North America/Europe–only audiences, AWS/Azure often have broader service coverage and integrations.

### Is Alibaba Cloud cheaper than AWS?
It can be **more cost-effective in many Asia regions**, especially with subscription discounts and promotions. Always compare the same region + instance family + bandwidth + storage.

### Do I need an ICP license to host a website in Mainland China?
In most cases, **yes**. If you host content inside Mainland China, ICP filing/licensing and related compliance steps are commonly required.

### Which region should I choose for China-facing users?
Common choices are **Mainland China regions** (with compliance requirements) or **Hong Kong** for simpler setup with good China adjacency. The best choice depends on latency targets and compliance constraints.

### Is OSS comparable to Amazon S3?
OSS is Alibaba’s object storage and is broadly comparable to S3 for common workloads (static assets, backups, logs). Feature parity varies by region and product tier.

### Is Alibaba Cloud suitable for WordPress?
Yes. For WordPress, a small ECS plus a separated DB (or managed DB) is a common setup. Choose a region close to your visitors and avoid under-provisioning RAM.

### What’s the biggest downside for beginners?
The console can feel complex, and **English documentation** may not be as exhaustive as AWS. If you want the simplest learning curve, providers like DigitalOcean can be easier.

### Can I use Alibaba Cloud for cross-border e-commerce?
Yes — it’s a common fit for **China-facing** or **Asia expansion** e-commerce, especially when combined with CDN/acceleration and the regional network.
