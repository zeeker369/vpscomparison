## Quick intro

This region hub groups all **Africa** data center pages on this site.

- Start with the **Data Centers** index and filter by region, provider, and China picks.
- Then open a specific metro page (e.g., Johannesburg, Cape Town) to compare providers.

## Suggested workflow

1. Open the Africa-filtered view: [Africa data centers](/datacenter/?region=africa)
2. Pick a metro and verify routing (MTR/traceroute) at peak hours
3. Confirm packet loss, jitter, and P95 latency before production

## Notes

For cross-border audiences, treat routing as the deciding factor—do not rely on geography alone.
