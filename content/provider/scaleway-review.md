# Scaleway Review (2026)

Scaleway is a European cloud infrastructure provider emphasizing flexibility, ARM compute support, and cost-efficient VPS options.  
It appeals to developers seeking alternative CPU architectures alongside traditional x86 and those targeting European workloads.

## Market Position

Scaleway differentiates itself with:

- ARM compute options unavailable on many peers  
- Strong price flexibility  
- European-first data centers  
- Simple billing and predictable costs  

Compared with DigitalOcean or UpCloud, Scaleway appeals when:

- ARM workloads are targeted  
- Cost control and EU latency matter
