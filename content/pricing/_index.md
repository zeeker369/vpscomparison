## What you'll find here

Most cloud bills are dominated by **networking and data movement**, not CPU cores.

- **Egress / bandwidth:** the hidden cost driver for APIs, downloads, and media sites.
- **Cross-zone / cross-region:** costs that appear after you enable HA by default.
- **CDN/edge strategies:** the easiest way to reduce origin egress.
